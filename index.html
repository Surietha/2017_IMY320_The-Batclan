My name is Shadow
Is a first person horror game.
The ultimate goal of the game,
shut down the compound
and lock the final door.
In order to lock this door ,
you need to collect 4 keys.
Do not expect it to be easy!
Lurking in the shadows
is a creature following you endlessly
Its instinct to kill you
its only weakness...light
